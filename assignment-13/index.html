My Trip to New Orleans

By Freya Gooday

In April of 2024 the Artist Collective went on our yearly club trip. This year we went to New, Orleans Louisiana. We were only there for three full days, but we were able to pack in a lot of activities.

In the menu bar above, you can see images from the New Orelans Museum of Art (NOMA), or pictures of some of the numerous animals I saw while there.
