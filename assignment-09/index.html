How to Screen Print

(With access to the UM Print Studio)

This instructible will guide you through every step of creating a screen print, from designing your image to printing it. There will be three parts: Designing Your Image, Preparing and Cleaning Your Screen, and Printing.

Supplies

Provided by the UM Print Shop

  • Newsprint
  • Printer paper
  • Oil
  • Scoop Coater
  • Photo emulsion remover
  • Printing rig
  • Clamps

What you will need to acquire

  • Opaque drawing utensils such as pigment pens and paint pens
  • Screen(s)
  • Photo emulsion
  • Clear Packing tape
  • Duct tape
  • Masking tape
  • Mylar sheet
  • Good paper for printing on
