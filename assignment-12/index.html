Origami Tutorial: Jumping Frog

Step 1

Fold and unfold your square sheet of paper in half.

Step 2

Fold and unfold each of the top two corners to make an 'x' with the creases.

Step 3

Fold and ufold the top of the rectangle down so that the crease lines up with the center of the 'x' made in Step 2.

Step 4

Using the creases as a guide, push in the sides and flatten so that the top forms a triangle.

Step 5

Fold up the bottom corners of the trangle so that they poke up past the edge of the paper.

Step 6

fold the bottom of the rectangle uo to meet the bottom of the triangle.

Step 7

Fold in the sides so that they meet in the middle.

Step 8

Fold up the bottom of the rectangle, similar to Step 6, but this time just crease and unfold.

Step 9

Pull up on the bottom inner corners of the rectangle to open it up. Fold as shown in the illustration. This may take some finessing, watch the video linked below if you are having trouble.

Step 10

Fold down the corners of the trapezoid.

Step 11

Zig-zag fold to make the jumping mecahnism. This means to fold first the top dotted line, and then the second dotted line the opposide direction. If you are having trouble understanding this step, check out the video linked below.

Step 12

Flip it over and youre done! You can draw a face on your frog if you want. To make it jump, press down on its back and slide your finger off. He may even do a flip!
