Scoloposcelis


From Ricky.


Scoloposcelis is a genus of minute pirate bugs in the family Lyctocoridae. There are about seven described species in Scoloposcelis.


Scoloposcelis

Scientific classification

Domain: Eukaryota

Kingdom: Animalia

Phylum: Anthropoda

Class: Insecta

Order: Hemiptera

Suborder: Heretoptera

Fmaily: Anthocoridae

Genus: Scoloposcelis

Species

These seven species belong to the genus Scoloposcelis:

  • Scoloposcelis basilica Drake and Harris, 1926
  • Scoloposcelis flavicornis Reuter, 1871
  • Scoloposcelis koreanus Jung & Yamada
  • Scoloposcelis obscurella (Zetterstedt, 1838)
  • Scoloposcelis parallela (Motschulsky, 1863)
  • Scoloposcelis pulchella (Zetterstedt, 1838)
  • Scoloposcelis seidaii
